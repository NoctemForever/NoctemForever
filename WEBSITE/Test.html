About Us

Welcome to NoctemForever, a visionary capital company that thrives on the pulse of the night. With a portfolio as diverse as the spectrum of twilight, we bring together a collection of enterprises that redefine nocturnal experiences.

At the heart of our empire lies Nocturnal, an electrifying rave event that ignites the darkness with pulsating beats and euphoric vibes. We are the architects of unforgettable nights, where boundaries blur and souls unite under the spell of music and rhythm.

Venture into the rebellious spirit of Vngel, our punk clothing company that embodies the essence of raw individuality and unapologetic expression. From the streets to the stage, Vngel's edgy designs empower the nocturnal tribe to stand out and make a statement.

Ascend to the heights of the Vngelhill, a festival event like no other, where the spirit of rebellion converges with the euphoria of music. Here, amidst the wild and untamed landscape, we celebrate freedom, creativity, and the unyielding spirit of the night. We would love to see you at the hill.

Indulge in the intoxicating allure of Scythe, our signature alcohol brand that tantalizes the senses and fuels the nocturnal adventure. Crafted with precision and passion, each sip is a journey into the depths of the night, where inhibitions fade and spirits soar.

At NoctemForever, we are not merely stakeholders; we are the architects of nocturnal dreams, weaving together experiences that transcend the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary. Join us as we paint the canvas of the night with bold strokes of passion, creativity, and boundless energy. Welcome to the realm where the night reigns eternal. .
